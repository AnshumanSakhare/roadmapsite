## 🔄 How to Choose

Ask yourself:

1. **What energizes you?**
   - Building? → App Dev
   - Optimizing? → Engineer
   - Discovering? → Researcher
   - Connecting? → Product

2. **What's your background?**
   - Web/mobile dev? → App Dev
   - CS/infrastructure? → Engineer
   - PhD/research? → Researcher
   - Business/sales? → Product

3. **What's your goal?**
   - Startup founder? → App Dev + Product
   - CTO or lead engineer? → Engineer + Researcher
   - AI researcher? → Researcher
   - PM at tech company? → Product

4. **How much time do you have?**
   - 8 weeks? → App Dev
   - 12 weeks? → Engineer or Researcher
   - 16+ weeks? → Hybrid path
